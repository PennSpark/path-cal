Path Cal

This is a Google Extension that allows you to download (ical) or upload (GCal) your Penn schedule from your primary cart!

Instructions:

  1. Login to your Path @ Penn Page
  2. Click on your primary cart!
  3. Click on the extension, it should ask you to save your classes
  4. Choose one of the options below to download your calendar (ical) or upload to google calendar!
